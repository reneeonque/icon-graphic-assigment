"In 2007, New York Legislature passed a new law called the Contracts for Excellence (C4E)."

"NYC submitted a 5-yr plan to reduce class sizes to no more than 23 for grades 4-8."

"4th-8th [grade] class sizes...are now larger than the C4E goal in 2019 by 3.4 students per class."

It's apparent from the data above that the class sizes in District 30 are only getting larger, despite the C4E goal that was set over ten years ago. District 30 is already an overcrowded district that lacks space for students. If class sizes continue to grow, the circumstances will only get worse, especially during a pandemic.
